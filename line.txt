


Clickadu Multi-format Ad Network For Publishers Review: Conclusion 


Publishers looking for a multi-format ad network should definitely check out Clickadu. The network has a wide range of ad formats and a good variety of targeting options. They also have a very user-friendly interface which makes creating and managing ads a breeze. In addition, Clickadu is one of the few ad networks that offer pop-under ads. Overall, we were very impressed with Clickadu and would definitely recommend them to publishers looking for a quality multi-format ad network.
