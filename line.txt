


The starter Core plan costs $29 per month, the Create plan is $45 per month, Automate is $59 per month, and the Suite plan is $209 per month. All plans come with a 14-day free trial; no credit card required. Each plan includes features, such as unlimited landing pages, technical support, AWS cloud hosting, drag and drop landing page builder, and more.


The Core plan is best for solopreneurs and small businesses who want to create a professional website and grow their business online. The Create plan is best for businesses who want to create more advanced marketing campaigns, such as email marketing and A/B testing. The Automate plan is best for businesses who want to automate their marketing tasks, such as email marketing and lead capture. The Suite plan is best for businesses who want to have all the features of the Core, Create, and Automate
