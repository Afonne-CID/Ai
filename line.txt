


How To Get Started With AWeber


Now that you know all about AWeber, it’s time to get started!

First, you’ll need to create an account. You can do this by clicking the “Create Account” button on our homepage.

Once you have created your account, you’ll be taken to the AWeber dashboard. This is where you can create your first email campaign.

To create a campaign, click the “Create a New Campaign” button.

You’ll then be asked to choose a campaign type. We recommend choosing the “Regular Campaign” type.

Next, you’ll be asked to enter your campaign’s name and email subject.

You’ll then be asked to create your email content.

AWeber offers a variety of templates to choose from, or you can create your own email from scratch.

Once you have created your email, you’ll need to decide who will receive it.

You can choose to send your email to a list of subscribers, or you can send it to a specific group of people.

To send your email to a list of subscribers, you’ll need to create a list.

To create a list, click the “Lists” tab and then click the “Create a New List” button.
