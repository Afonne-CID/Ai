


What Is Media.net's RPM: Media.net typically pays about $5/1,000 impression, which is among the best in the industry. You can typically run 3 ads per page, meaning if you have 334 views, that's about 1,000 impressions. At $5 RPM you can make $5/day or roughly $150/mo off only 334 visits/day.


Media.net is a contextual ad network, which places ads on websites based on the content of the page. For example, if you visit a website about cars, you may see a car ad. Media.net is one of the largest contextual ad networks, and as a result, can offer competitive rates.

Media.net's RPM (revenue per 1,000 impressions) is typically around $5. This means that for every 1,000 impressions (views of your ad), you will earn $5. This is a competitive rate, and as a result, Media.net is one of the most popular ad networks.
