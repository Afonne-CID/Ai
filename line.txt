


Media.net traffic requirements: Your content needs to be in native English. The majority of the traffic source needs to come from the US, UK, and Canada.. A significant amount of premium content needs to be featured on the website.


Media.net is an ad network that operates on a Cost-Per-Click (CPC) basis. This means that publishers are paid based on the number of clicks that their ads receive. In order to be eligible to participate in the Media.net network, your website must meet the following requirements:

The content on your website must be in native English.

The majority of your traffic must come from the United States, the United Kingdom, or Canada.

You must have a significant amount of premium content on your website.

If you meet these requirements, you can apply to join the Media.net network.
