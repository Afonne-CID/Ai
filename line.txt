


How To Get Started With AWeber


AWeber is one of the most popular email marketing platforms on the market. It’s easy to use, and it offers a wide range of features that make it an attractive option for small businesses and bloggers.

In this guide, we’re going to show you how to get started with AWeber. We’ll walk you through the steps of creating an account, adding subscribers, and sending out your first campaign.

Creating an Account

The first step is to create an account with AWeber. You can do this by clicking on the “Create Account” button on the AWeber website.

You’ll be asked to provide some information about your business, including your name, email address, and website.

Once you’ve filled out the form, click on the “Create My Account” button.

You’ll then be asked to verify your email address. Click on the link in the email that AWeber sends you, and you’ll be taken to the AWeber dashboard.

Adding Subscribers

The next step is to add subscribers to your list. You can do this by clicking on the “Add Subscribers” button in the AWeber dashboard.

You’ll be asked to provide some information about each subscriber, including their name, email address, and website.

You can also add subscribers manually by entering their email addresses in to the text box on the AWeber dashboard.

Sending a Campaign

The final step is to send a campaign. Click on the “Create a Campaign” button in the AWeber dashboard.

You’ll be asked to provide some information about your campaign, including the subject line, email content, and the date and time you want the campaign to be sent.

You can also add subscribers manually by entering their email addresses in to the text box on the AWeber dashboard.

Once you’ve filled out the form, click on the “Create Campaign” button.

AWeber will then send a test email to each of your subscribers. Once they’ve confirmed that they’ve received the email, your campaign will be sent out.
