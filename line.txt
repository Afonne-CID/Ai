


Is Campaign Monitor GDPR compliant?


The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a new EU data protection law that came into effect on May 25, 2018. The GDPR replaces the 1995 EU Data Protection Directive.

The GDPR applies to all organizations with EU or national customers, regardless of whether the organization has a physical presence in the EU.

Organizations that process the personal data of EU citizens must comply with the GDPR unless they can demonstrate that they meet certain conditions.

Organizations that process the personal data of EU citizens must implement specific measures to protect the personal data of EU citizens, including:

-Data encryption
-Data minimization
- pseudonymization
-Transparency
-Breach notification

Campaign Monitor is compliant with the GDPR. We have implemented specific measures to protect the personal data of EU citizens, including data encryption, data minimization, pseudonymization, and transparency.


Can you attach files in Campaign Monitor?


Yes! You can attach files to your Campaign Monitor emails by using the file upload field in the compose window. The maximum file size that you can attach is 10MB.
