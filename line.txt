How do I cancel Omnisend?


There are two ways you can cancel your Omnisend account:

1. You can cancel your account from your account settings.

2. You can email us at support@omnisend.com and we will be happy to help you cancel your account.

When you cancel your account, all your data will be permanently deleted and you will not be able to access it again.
