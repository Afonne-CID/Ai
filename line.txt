


How To Create a Publisher Account on SmartyAds


To create a publisher account on SmartyAds, you will need to provide some basic information about your company and contact details. Once you have registered, you will need to create an ad campaign and set up your targeting and budget.

SmartyAds is a self-service ad platform that allows you to create and manage your own ad campaigns. You can target your ads by country, region, age, gender, and interests, and you can set your budget and delivery schedule.

To create a publisher account on SmartyAds:

1. Go to https://www.smartyads.com and click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner of the page.

2. Enter your company information and contact details.

3. Click on "Create My Account".

4. Once your account is created, you will need to create an ad campaign.

5. To create an ad campaign, go to the "Campaigns" tab and click on "Create New Campaign".

6. Enter the details of your campaign and set your targeting and budget.

7. Click on "Create Campaign".

8. Once your campaign is created, you will need to set up your billing information.

9. To set up your billing information, go to the "Billing" tab and click on "Add New Payment Method".

10. Enter your payment information and click on "Save
