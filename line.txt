


Infolinks Application Requirements for Publishers


In order to be a publisher on the Infolinks platform, you must meet the following requirements:

- Have a website with a minimum of 500 visitors per day

- Have published original content on your website for a minimum of six months

- Use a minimum of three different ad units on your website

- Use a minimum of one Infolinks ad unit

- Use Google Analytics to track your website traffic

If you meet the above requirements, you can apply to become a publisher on the Infolinks platform.
