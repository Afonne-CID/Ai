


Cons of Free Web Hosting


There are several reasons why you might not want to use a free web hosting service. First, free web hosts typically place advertising on your website. This can be distracting or annoying to your visitors, and it can also reduce the amount of space you have to work with on your site. Additionally, free web hosts often have fewer features and options than paid hosting services. This can limit your ability to customize your site, and it may also prevent you from using certain plugins or software programs. Finally, free web hosting is not always reliable.
